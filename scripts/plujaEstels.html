What format is the most optimal for this type of image and why? The optimal format depends on the image type. For high-quality images with transparency, PNG is optimal. For complex images without transparency, JPEG can be more suitable due to better compression. GIF is good for simple animations. With what compression quality does the image not lose quality? The compression quality that maintains image quality varies. For JPEG, a compression level of 70-80% generally maintains a good balance between size and quality. Can we reduce its size even more without losing quality? You can reduce the size further with optimization tools, but extreme compression may reduce quality. What would happen if we save the image several times with JPEG format? If you save an image multiple times in JPEG format, it will undergo 'generation loss', as JPEG is a lossy format. Each save reduces the quality of the image.
